Selma Kamanya is not just a public figure. She is a builder. Her business ventures are not side projects they are the foundation of a long term vision for economic empowerment, cultural impact, and generational wealth. Every venture she is involved in is rooted in the same question: what does this create for Namibia?

Representing Namibia On The World Stage

Global Nation 1 is Selma's flagship platform built to bridge the gap between Namibian talent and global opportunity. GN1 exists because Selma saw a problem: Namibia has incredible talent that the world simply does not know about yet. GN1 changes that.

Through strategic cultural marketing, talent connections, and global collaborations, GN1 is putting Namibian creativity and culture on the world map one partnership at a time.

What GN1 Does:

  • Connects Namibian talent to global platforms and opportunities
  • Creates cultural marketing campaigns with international reach
  • Builds strategic partnerships between Namibian creatives and global brands
  • Positions Namibia as a creative and cultural force on the world stage

Building Wealth Through Property

Economic empowerment is not just a talking point for Selma it is something she is actively building. Through Okuyambekwa Properties, she is investing in Namibia's real estate landscape with a vision that goes beyond profit. Property ownership is one of the most powerful tools for generational wealth, and Selma is committed to making that tool more accessible for Namibians.

What Okuyambekwa Properties Stands For:

  • Sustainable property development
  • Economic empowerment through home ownership
  • Investment opportunities for Namibians
  • Long term wealth creation and financial independence
