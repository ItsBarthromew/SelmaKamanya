Privacy Policy

This page explains how information is handled on Selma Kamanya's website. The site is primarily a portfolio and contact hub, so most interactions happen when you choose to reach out through the linked social platforms or WhatsApp.

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Information You Share

We do not ask for account creation or detailed personal profiles on this website. If you choose to contact Selma through Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp, the information you send is handled through those third-party services.

How Information Is Used

Any information you share is used only to respond to inquiries, manage bookings, and continue conversations about collaborations, speaking, partnerships, or media requests.

Cookies And Analytics

This website does not intentionally collect analytics, advertising data, or login information through its own forms. External services you open from the site may have their own cookies and privacy practices.

External Links

Links to Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, and other pages leave this website. Please review the privacy policies of those services if you want to understand how they process your information.

Contact

For questions about this privacy page or the website, use the social links in the footer or the contact options shown on the home page.

Quick contact: Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp are the fastest ways to reach the team behind the site.
